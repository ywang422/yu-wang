About

I lead US content ecology team at TikTok , where we build and deploy large-scale, production-ready intelligent systems for short-video understanding and distribution. Our work spans large language models, natural language processing, computer vision, multimodal representation learning, and recommender systems. Focusing on short video as a high-dimensional, multimodal, and creator-driven data format, my team systematically tackles core algorithmic challenges across content understanding, creator understanding, and inspiration-driven distribution. We provide a unified and reusable algorithmic foundation that supports multiple core TikTok products.
